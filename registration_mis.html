Pilipino Program (4Ps)

Set 14 Registration

DSWD MIMAROPA is currently conducting validation and registration for new potential beneficiaries from the North District of Oriental Mindoro through the 2026 CBMS.

Who are the Potential Beneficiaries?

To be eligible for the 4Ps Set 14 in the North District, a household must meet the following criteria.

Classified as Poor

Identified as 'poor' or 'near-poor' based on your municipality's 2026 Community-Based Monitoring System (CBMS) database.

Children Aged 0-18

The household must have at least one child aged 0 to 18 years old at the time of registration.

Pregnant Member

Alternatively, eligible if there is a pregnant woman in the household during the assessment.

Note: Inclusion in the CBMS database does not mean automatic cash grants. Strict validation must be completed at the DSWD offices.

The 5-Step Registration Process

An organized process coordinated with the LGUs of the North District (Puerto Galera, San Teodoro, Baco, Calapan City, Naujan, Victoria, Socorro, Pola).

1

Masterlist Posting

Check the official list at your Barangay Hall or Municipal/City Link office (e.g., Calapan City Hall or municipal halls).

2

Community Assembly

Attend the scheduled Community Assembly in your area to understand the 4Ps conditionalities and benefits.

3

Assessment & Validation

DSWD Social Workers will conduct interviews using the PhilSys/National ID to verify household information.

4

Submission of Requirements

Submit necessary documents at the One-Stop Shop registration sites organized by the Mindoro LGUs.

5

Oath of Commitment (Kasunduan)

Sign the Oath of Commitment, signifying your agreement to fulfill the health and education conditionalities.

What to prepare and bring?

Prepare these documents and bring them during validation. Complete requirements will speed up your registration.

Reminders:

  • • Bring original copies for verification and photocopies for submission.
  • • A One-Stop Shop is available in North District municipalities, assisted by the LGUs.

Documentary Requirements

  • PhilSys ID (National ID) or any valid government-issued ID
  • Birth Certificates (PSA or Local Civil Registrar) of all household members
  • Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • Marriage Certificate (if applicable)
  • Health Records (Vaccination booklet for ages 0-5)
  • Medical Certificate confirming pregnancy (for pregnant members)
  • School Enrollment Records / Form 137 (for children aged 3-18)

Program Conditionalities

Beneficiaries must comply with these conditions to continuously receive cash grants.

Health & Nutrition

  • Pregnant women must avail of pre- and post-natal care.
  • Children 0-5 years old must receive regular check-ups and vaccines.
  • Children 1-14 years old must be dewormed twice a year.

Education

  • Children 3-18 years old must be enrolled in school.
  • Must maintain at least 85% monthly class attendance.

Family Development

  • Parents must attend the monthly Family Development Sessions (FDS).
  • Active participation in community activities.
